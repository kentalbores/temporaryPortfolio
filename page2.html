“The “joy of discovery” is one of the fundamental joys of play itself. Not just the joy of discovering secrets within the game, but also the joy of uncovering the creator’s vision. It’s that “Aha!” moment where it all makes sense, and behind the world the player can feel the touch of another creative mind. In order for it to be truly joyful, however, it must remain hidden from plain view—not carved as commandments into stone tablets but revealed, piece by piece, through the player’s exploration of the game’s rules.”
― Derek Yu, Spelunky

“The designer's real goal is to enrich the player's internal experiences. That goal is harder to achieve, and it's damned difficult to measure. But it's the truth. And pursuing that truth makes your designs smaller, simpler, more focused, and more elegant than they could ever get by strategy counting.”
― Tynan Sylvester, Designing Games

Achieving goals is an important part of leading a fulfilling and successful life. Setting goals helps us to focus our efforts, stay motivated, and make progress towards what we want to achieve. However, achieving goals is not always easy and requires dedication, discipline, and a plan.

One key to achieving goals is to set specific and achievable targets. This means breaking larger goals down into smaller, more manageable tasks, and setting deadlines for each step. This helps to keep you on track and motivated, and allows you to track your progress and see the progress you are making.

Another important aspect of achieving goals is to stay motivated and focused. This means regularly reminding yourself of the benefits of achieving your goals and rewarding yourself for making progress. It is also important to stay positive, even when faced with setbacks or obstacles. Remember, achieving your goals is a journey and it is important to be patient and persistent. With hard work and determination, you can achieve any goal you set for yourself.
